ABOUT COTTRADEVIZ

WHAT?

COTTradeViz was born as my portfolio project as part of my journey to become a software engineer at Holberton School. I embarked on this endeavor because I recognized the intricacies of the forex and financial markets, particularly the profound influence of institutional trading activities. To align my trading strategies with these institutions and improve my market success, I sought a tool that would provide valuable insights. That's when I discovered the Commitment of Traders (COT) data.

WHY?

COTTradeViz distinguishes itself with unique features such as a live ticker displaying real-time net position data for various instruments. We provide educational resources to help users understand the significance of COT data. Our platform also offers in-depth breakdowns of reports by class of institutions, from commercial to non-commercial. Users can perform historical analyses to inform their trading strategies.

HOW?

The primary goal of COTTradeViz is to become the go-to tool for traders seeking insights from COT data and institutional trading. To achieve this, we focus on creating a visually appealing and detailed platform that empowers traders with essential information.

WHO?

COTTradeViz caters to a diverse audience, including both novice and experienced individual traders. We aim to provide valuable resources for learning and decision-making. Additionally, our platform is designed to serve institutional investors seeking data-driven insights.
